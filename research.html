Research Interest

I am a mathematician specializing in differential geometry. My research focuses on variational problems in geometry, including the analysis of elastic curves and minimal surfaces. I am particularly interested in the stability and index theory of geometric objects, as well as their behavior in different ambient spaces. In addition, I am enthusiastic about formal proof verification and am actively working on formalization projects using Lean 4.
